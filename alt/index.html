Welcome to the personal page and playground of "Derjyn". Here you can view my previous projects, take a peek at my laboratory, and find some links to cool places around the net related to design, coding, or bits I find pure awesome.

About the design

I am a sucker for pixel based design, retro 8 and 16bit schemes... The nostalgia of the old days has never gone away for me. When I approached this personal project, I went in several different directions at first, until deciding that the site was just that: personal. Therefore, I needed to actually look inside for a vision that truly left me satisfied.

There is still work needed before I am truly happy with everything though. Much more needs to be done to make the site standards compliant and responsive. More tweaks need to be made on the performance end. There are also plans for adding some "easter eggs", because I really enjoy user discovery in code and design. In the end, this is my playground and I will certainly be playing. Enjoy.
